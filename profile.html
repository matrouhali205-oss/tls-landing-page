The Layer Studio is a precision drafting and architectural services firm based in Toronto. We work exclusively with contractors, real estate professionals, and homeowners who need fast, accurate, professional drawings — without the 3-week wait.

We started TLS because we saw how much time and money was being lost on back-and-forth with traditional drafting firms — slow turnarounds, unclear scopes, and drawings that didn't match what was actually needed on site. We built a better process.

Every project that comes through our door gets the same treatment: a clear scope conversation, a defined timeline, and drawings that are ready to submit. No revision traps. No surprise fees. Just clean work, delivered on time.
